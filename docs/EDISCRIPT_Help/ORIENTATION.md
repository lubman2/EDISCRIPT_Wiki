# ORIENTATION

sets orientation of printing: portrait or landscape.

### Format:

## ORIENTATION o

o 0 = portrait, 1 = landscape

### Example:

ORIENTATION 1

	PRINTOPEN

	TEXT 10 30 "Test"

	PRINTPREVIEW

	PRINTCLOSE
